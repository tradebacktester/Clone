Now build the Executive Judgment & Decision Simulation Engine.
The objective is to allow KRYTOS to evaluate multiple possible decisions before taking action and select the most evidence-supported course while remaining completely explainable.
This module does not predict the future.
Instead, it compares realistic alternatives using available intelligence and historical evidence.
Objective
Before every trade, Executive AI must simulate multiple possible decisions.
It should ask:
Should I trade now?
Should I wait?
Should I reduce risk?
Should I ignore this setup?
Should I observe the market longer?
Which decision has historically produced the best outcome under similar conditions?
The chosen action must maximize long-term robustness rather than short-term profit.

Rules
The Executive Judgment Engine MAY:
Simulate multiple decision paths.
Rank alternatives.
Recommend the strongest option.
Learn from counterfactual analysis.
Improve future decision quality.
It must NEVER:
Predict future prices as certainty.
Rewrite the deterministic strategy.
Ignore Executive Risk decisions.
Deploy experimental research.
Bypass safety or approval workflows.
The Executive Judgment Engine exists to ensure that every trade is selected only after objectively comparing all reasonable alternatives using evidence, historical performance, and transparent reasoning.
